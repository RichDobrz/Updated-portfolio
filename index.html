Swords And Shields

Swords and Shields is a 1v1 King of the Hill Style Game. Players are placed into a queue where the first two players in line battle it out to be King. Winner stays, losers goes to the back of the line to wait for another chance.

Technologies Used: ReactJS, NodeJS, Express, SocketIO, MongoDB

Lets Go Biking

Lets Go Biking is an app for bike routing. Plot a start and end point for your ride and a new route will be generated. Lets go Biking also provides current weather information and a list of points of interest along the generated route.

Technologies Used: Jquery, Bootstrap4, HERE API, Open Weather Map
